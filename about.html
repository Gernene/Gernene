About Gernene

Hi there! My name is Gernene Tan. You may also recognize me as Hwona (my pen name). I'm a high school junior who's looking to study web design, cyber security, and computer science. I started creating websites and digital art seven years ago - and I have to say that I love what I do!

As a young girl, I was fascinated by the idea of utilizing code to add a whole new layer of depth and interaction to my artistic creations. I have since worked with HTML/CSS, PHP and MySQL, Javascript, Ruby on Rails, Python, and Java - Not to mention that I continue to learn more! My various projects range from simple static pages to full-blown ecommerce sites, giving me a wide range of experience with different programming languages, tools, and platforms.

Today, my goal is to develop my design, technical, and business skillsets to maximize the quality of my work and the efficiency of my service. A learning student, I'm always on the lookout for new opportunities that pass my way. Do take a look around my portfolio and ask any questions...

And remember me when you hunt for your next web designer!
